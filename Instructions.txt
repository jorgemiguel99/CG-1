1) generator / Generator:

	.Plano: "generator plane <length> <width> plane.3d"
	.Caixa: "generator box <length> <width> <height> box.3d"
	.Esfera: "generator sphere <radius> <slices> <stacks> sphere.3d"
	.Cone: "generator cone <radius> <height> <slices> <stacks> cone.3d"

2) Ficheiros 3d:
 
	.generator plane 2 4 plane.3d
	.generator box 2 4 3 box.3d
	.generator sphere 1 30 30 sphere.3d
	.generator cone 1 2 30 30 cone.3d

3) read <filename.3d> / Read <filename.3d>
	
	.read plane.3d ou Read plane.3d
	.read box.3d ou Read box.3d
	.read sphere.3d ou Read sphere.3d
	.read cone.3d ou Read cone.3d
